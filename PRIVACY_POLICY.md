# Privacy Policy — WY Resource Map

**Last updated:** February 23, 2026

## Overview

The WY Resource Map mobile application ("App") is developed and maintained by Casper Youth Hub. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
When you tap "Near Me," the App requests permission to access your device's approximate location. This data is used **only** to sort resources by proximity and is **never** transmitted to our servers or any third party. Location access is entirely optional — the App functions fully without it.

### Saved Resources
Resources you save are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

### Cached Data
The App temporarily caches resource listings on your device to improve performance. This cache is automatically refreshed and contains only publicly available resource information.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
- We do **not** create user accounts
- We do **not** share any data with third parties
- We do **not** use cookies

## Third-Party Services

The App connects to the Wyoming Youth Resource Map API (casperyouthhubmap.org) to fetch publicly available resource listings. No personal data is sent in these requests.

When you tap external links (directions, phone numbers, websites, Google Forms), you are directed to third-party services (Apple Maps, Google Maps, phone dialer, web browser) which have their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

The App is designed to help young people ages 11-20 find resources. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

## Data Retention

All data stored by the App (saved resources, cached listings) resides only on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all stored data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
